Frequently Asked Questions

We are here to help you with any questions you may have. Here are some of the most common questions we get asked.

1. How do I know if my roof needs repair or replacement?

Look for signs like missing or damaged Tiles, slates, leaks, water stains on ceilings or walls, sagging areas in a flat roof, and excessive granules in gutters. A professional inspection can provide a clear assessment.

2. What type of roofing material is best for my home?

The best material depends on your budget, climate, home style, and durability needs. Common options include asphalt shingles, metal roofing, slate, clay tiles, and synthetic materials.

3. How long does a typical roofing project take?

Most roof replacements take days or weeks, depending on the roof size, material, weather, and complexity of the job. Repairs may take just a few hours.

4. What is the average lifespan of a roof?

The lifespan varies based on the material:

  • Asphalt shingles: 20-30 years
  • Metal: 40-70 years
  • Slate or tile: 50-100+ years
  • Flat roofs: 10-20 years

    • 5. How can I maintain my roof to extend its lifespan?

    Regular maintenance includes cleaning gutters, removing debris, inspecting for damage after storms, and scheduling professional inspections annually. Promptly addressing small issues can prevent major problems and extend the roof's life.
